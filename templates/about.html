Hi, I'm Zach!

(and these are my dogs :))

I am a Portland based digital product designer and strategist. I have an educational background in history and extensive career experience in a SaaS Start-Up. I offer a lot of experience in small business strategy, marketing, sales, customer support and everything in between. Strategizing how to utilize an extremely finite amount of resources to maximize a small company’s potential has been a large part of my young career. Now, I am looking forward to applying the same principles to designing digital experiences.

In my free time, I enjoy playing with my dogs, snowboarding, staying active, reading, and working on personal projects.

I was born and raised in Los Angeles, but I moved to the Pacific North West to attend Reed College.

At Reed I got my B.A in History, after I completed a year-long independent project on the history of America’s Federal Narcotics laws and their use as tools to subjugate minority communities in the United States.

After graduation, I was hired as the second employee at a local SaaS start-up. Being such a small company, I had the unique opportunity to take on many roles, but I quickly discovered a passion for UX/UI strategy and design. Now, I am working hard to expand my knowledge and concept creative solutions that are useful to everyone.
