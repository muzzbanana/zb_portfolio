Hi, I'm Zach!

I am a Portland based Digital Product Designer and Strategist. I offer experience in UX/UI design, small business strategy, marketing, sales, and customer support. Concepting how to utilize finite temporal and financial resources to maximize a small company’s potential has been a large part of my young career. Now, I am looking forward to applying the same principles to designing digital experiences.

In my personal time, I enjoy playing with my dogs, snowboarding, reading, and working on personal projects. I am a history buff with an insatiable curiosity that keeps me endlessly busy.

I was born and raised in Los Angeles. In 2015, I moved to the Pacific North West to attend Reed College.

I received my B.A. in History from Reed in 2018, after I completed a year-long independent project on the history of America’s Federal Narcotics laws.

That subsequent fall, I was hired as the second employee at a local SaaS start-up. Being such a small company, I had the unique opportunity to take on many roles, but I quickly discovered a passion for UX/UI strategy and design. Now, I am working hard to expand my knowledge and concept creative solutions that are useful to everyone.
